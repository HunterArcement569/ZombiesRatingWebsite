Introducing a new rating system that takes into account the foundational game mechanics each map is built on.

The Rating System

Call of Duty Zombies has over 70 zombies maps, and not all of them are equal. The foundation of every map is the game engine and mechanics, from old-school 4 perk limits, to differing point systems, from modern health systems, to graphic styling. The game a map is on plays a pivotal role in how well or poorly that map is viewed by a player. Imagine your favorite zombies map, now put that map on your least favorite game of the series. Does that map still hold up, or do the mechanics drop the map's rating a tier or two? This new rating system uses the map rating as a 0-to-1 multiplier against the individual map ratings to come up with a final rating.
